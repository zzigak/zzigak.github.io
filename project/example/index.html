Slides Follow
Photo by rawpixel on Unsplash

We are a group of prospective students from DGM whose goal is to eliminate one source of stress on students, school work. Helping with commonly hardest classes, science classes, we strive to better the welfare of our fellow classmates.

By offering online and in-person consultations we aim to break barriers like residence location, financial capability, and time that restrict students from getting help with their educational problems. Flexible timetables, free tutoring services, and online options allow our students to choose the time of tutoring more freely.

Our non-profit student-run educational organization is striving to provide free, one on one, online classes & tutoring in English and Slovene to everyone internationally no matter their socio-economic status, their beliefs, religion, race, and gender.
